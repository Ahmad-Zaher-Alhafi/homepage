About me
What Sets Me Apart as a Developer?

I utilize the Unity engine and C# programming language, along with Git for version control, to ensure efficient collaboration and project management. My experience includes working with a diverse, international team, enriching our creative process.

My passion for game development began in my childhood when I spent my free time immersed in games and creating my own. This enthusiasm drives my commitment to the craft.

Looking ahead, I aspire to become a game development lead, where I can blend my technical skills and leadership abilities to guide teams in creating engaging and innovative games. I welcome connections and opportunities to discuss my work further!
